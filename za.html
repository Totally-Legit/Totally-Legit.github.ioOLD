Dwarfs and elves are remarkably similar!

Only recently did the humans make the elves much taller and less Chistmasy in fiction.

We dwarfs actually invented a machine to inherit the powers of the elves, and it worked.

We called it the

--Wackifying

--Intricate

--Instrument

It took in small disks of hardlight which represented the spells of the elves.

It was quite fun if I do say so.

But its power was too great, so we locked it and the disks away on the deepest story of the caverns.

Perhaps there remains something to be found the those vast libraries of disks.

But that is enough musing for today.
