Anchient Scribbles

I must write this quickly as I am hiding for my life this very moment.

Someone must know of my fate.

My name is Gob. I was forging for puddleglooms(those lovely, lovely shrooms) when suddenly I stumbled and fell into a cave.

The cave was huge and brightly lit, but rough and untamed unlike dwarf caves.

In one corner was a pile of small pouches and in the other was a terrible, slumbering beast!

I tip=toed over to the pouches. They were small and would barely fit on my FOOT.

Then I found tiny little beasts sleeping in them!

I picked up as many as I could and ran, the beast now chasing after me.

Beware reader! Do not, I repeat, DO NOT go and find these strange pouches.

I SWEAR nothing good will come of it.
