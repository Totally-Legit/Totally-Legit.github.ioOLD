Date: #67@#&^78^#2768

The dwarfs think they invented their magic, but little do they know that we elves gifted it to them.

There will come a time when we retake it.

In the mean while, I happen to have stolen one of their magic circles of hardlight

Though, not from the drawfs, but from my father!

I found it in his bookshelf in the great hall with the tree. It seemed a little out of place.

It said the most horrific things to me!

But enough of that, Santa has need of me.

I must finish 50 presents by Christmas! O poor is me.

Good night journel.
